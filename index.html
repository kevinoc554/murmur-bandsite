Tour announcement - 10/02/22

We know you guys have been waiting patiently for an announcement, so as your reward this is a big one!

We are happy to announce the first round of dates for our Pilgrimage Ireland & UK tour. Check out our tour section for info on specific dates, and links to ticket sellers. Don't see your hometown there? Be sure to subscribe to our newsletter to keep up to date on the latest announcements.

Stay tuned for more news, dates, and a first look at the lead single off Pilgrimage in the coming days!

Love, as always,

Murmur

New Music - 20/01/22

We are happy to announce the imminent release of our new record Pilgrimage.

Releasing on February 21st, Pilgrimage marks a return to the indie sound pallette found on early records like 2007's Journey.

Recorded over the course of 2021 in Lorem Ipsum Studio, with legendary sound engineer Guy Producer at the helm, Pilgrimage represents a true snapshot of both where we are coming from and where we aim to go. We are excited to show you more closer to the date, so stay tuned for further updates.

Love, as always,

Murmur

New Year, New Us - 01/01/22

Well, it's a new year, so it's as good a time as any to take stock, reflect on the past and look to the future.

We'd like to take this opportuntity to thank all our fans for supporting us over these last few difficult years. Lots of industries have suffered and live music is no different. Not being able to get out on the road and share our music with you guys has been a real struggle. However, we're hoping to have some good news on that front very soon, so keep your eyes peeled here and be sure to check the tour section regularly.

That's all for this update, but rest assured that more news (and some surprises) are yet to come!

Happy New Year and love, as always

Murmur
